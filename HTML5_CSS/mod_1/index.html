Boudin au pommes - Recette

This is my favorite recipe for 3 people.

List of Ingredients:

  • 3 Boudin
  • 3 apples

Steps:

  1. Boil some water and in the meantime peal the apples and then cut hem in small pieces.
  2. Put the apples in pieces in the boiling water and let it cook for 40min.
  3. Cook the Boudin when there is 10min remaining for the apples
  4. Enjoy!
