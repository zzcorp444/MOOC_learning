Vegetable Soup

This is a hearty, homemade vegetarian friendly vegetable soup that I've been perfecting since I went to high school.

Ingredients

  • 2 diced yellow onions
  • 1 Leek, Chopped
  • 4 Carrots chopped and halved
  • 4 stalks of celery chopped
  • 2 potatoes, cubed and soaked overnight
  • 1 bag frozen mixed vegetables
  • Knorr vegetable boullian
  • White pepper
  • Italian herbs

Directions

  1. Soak potatoes overnight
  2. Add 6 cups of water to a pot and bring to a boil
  3. Add potatoes, onions, carrots and celery to pot and reduce to simmer
  4. Let simmer for 30 mins
  5. Next add the remaining ingredients and let soup sit until flavors homogeonize

Salad

A delicious salad

Ingredients

  • Lettuce or green leafy vegetable of your choice
  • Assorted other vegetables i.e. onions, tomatoes, bell peppers, radishes.
  • Dressing of your choice, I chose italian but it's really up to you!
  • Cheese if you so desire

Directions

  1. Chop up vegetables into appropriate salad sizes--think about how you will pick it up with your fork.
  2. Toss all of the vegetables and the cheese together.
  3. You can also toss the salad in the dressing but depending on your family or the group you are serving it may be better served on the side.

Nora's Famous Cranberry Sauce

This is my favorite cranberry sauce recipe. As a child I only had canned cranberry sauce and I hated it. Cranberry sauce is easy to make and superior to the canned cranberry found in stores.

Ingredients

  • Cranberries
  • Orange Juice
  • Sugar
  • Orange Zest

Directions

  1. Boil water, orange juice, and sugar in a medium sized pan.
  2. Once water and sugar have boiled to become a syrup add cranberries and orange zest.
  3. Simmer until cranberries burst.
  4. Cool until served
